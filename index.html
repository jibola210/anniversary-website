Dear Praise,

Happy First Anniversary, my love. Today marks one whole year since our story officially began, and honestly, it's difficult to put into words just how much this year has meant to me. Three hundred and sixty-five days may sound like just a number to some people, but to me, each day represents another memory, another lesson, another smile, another prayer, and another reason why I'm grateful that God brought you into my life.

Before you, I never imagined that one person could become such an important part of my everyday life. Yet somehow, you did. You became someone I look forward to talking to, someone whose happiness genuinely matters to me, someone who makes ordinary days feel special simply by being a part of them.

We've shared conversations that made us laugh until we couldn't stop smiling. We've had moments that tested us. We've had days when things were easy and days when they weren't. Through all of it, we kept choosing each other, and that's something I'll never take for granted.

Thank you for your patience whenever I wasn't perfect. Thank you for believing in me even when I doubted myself. Thank you for encouraging me to keep growing, to keep dreaming, and to keep becoming a better man. You may not always realize it, but you've influenced my life in more ways than you know.

One of the things I love most about us is that our relationship isn't just built on exciting moments. It's built on consistency, understanding, forgiveness, friendship and love. Those are the things that truly last.

As I worked on this website, I realized something. Every section, every card, every animation, every line of code reminded me of you. I wasn't building just another website. I was creating something that tells our story, something that you can always come back to and remember how our first year together felt.

This isn't the most expensive gift I could give you, but it's one of the most personal. I spent hours building it because you're worth the effort. Every little detail was created with you in mind.

As we begin our second year together, I pray that God continues to guide us, protect us, strengthen our love, and help us become everything He wants us to be. I pray we never lose the friendship that brought us together, the respect we've built, or the love we've continued to grow.

Thank you for loving me. Thank you for trusting me. Thank you for being my peace. Thank you for being my answered prayer. Thank you for simply being you.

If I had the chance to go back to the day our journey began, knowing everything I know now, I would still choose you without hesitation. And if tomorrow asked me to choose again... I'd still choose you. Every single time.

Happy First Anniversary, my beautiful girl. Here's to one amazing year behind us... and, by God's grace, a lifetime still ahead.

With all my love,

JIBOLA ❤️
